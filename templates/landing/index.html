Frequently asked.

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Do my customers need to download anything? +
No. StorefrontBot works entirely inside WhatsApp. Your customers just message your business number like normal — the bot handles the rest automatically. No apps, no signups, no friction.
How long does setup take? +
Most businesses are live within 30 minutes. You'll connect your WhatsApp Business number, set up your services and products in the dashboard, and your bot is ready to go. No coding required on the Starter and Growth plans.
What happens when someone messages outside business hours? +
The bot runs 24/7 — it never sleeps. Whether it's 2am or a public holiday, your customers get instant replies, can book appointments, and check stock availability. You review everything from the dashboard when you're ready.
Can I customise what the bot says? +
Yes. On Growth and Enterprise plans you can fully customise the bot's replies, menu options, service names, and tone. The Starter plan includes a default set of templates that covers most use cases out of the box.
Can I resell StorefrontBot to my own clients? +
Absolutely. The Enterprise plan includes white-labelling — you can rebrand the dashboard with your own logo and colors and resell it as your own product. Many of our customers use this as a standalone income stream.
What WhatsApp API does it use? +
StorefrontBot works with both Twilio's WhatsApp Sandbox (great for testing and early-stage businesses) and Meta's official WhatsApp Business Platform (recommended for production). We handle the integration — you just connect your number.
